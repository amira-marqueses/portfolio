SDCAmpusSpace

SDCAmpusSpace is a mobile application designed for students of St. Dominic College of Asia. It allows users to view available and occupied rooms, check upcoming events, and browse scheduled seminars. Students can also fill out and submit reservation forms for specific rooms and dates. Additionally, the app provides access to surveys, making campus activities more organized and accessible in one platform.

Purple Bean Coffee Shop Menu

Purple Bean Coffee Shop Menu is a mobile application where customers can browse different categories like coffee, pastries, and snacks. Users can easily add items to their cart, and the app will automatically display a summary of their order along with the total price. This makes ordering quick, convenient, and organized.
